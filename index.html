Frequenlty Asked Questions

CogCheck™ is designed to help you evaluate your cognitive wellness and identify any potential issues from the early stage.

We believe that knowledge is the first step to better health. That’s why we’ve created our comprehensive heart health questionnaire to help you understand your risk factors and take charge of your well-being. contact us for more info.

Why Take the Questionnaire? Our questionnaire is designed to provide you with insights into your heart health based on your lifestyle, medical history, and symptoms. By answering a few simple questions, you can: Gain a clearer understanding of your current health status. Identify potential risk factors for heart disease or stroke. Learn about steps you can take to improve your heart health.

How to Complete the Questionnaire: Read each question carefully and select the answer that best applies to you. Each answer has a score associated with it — no need to calculate it yourself! Submit your responses, and our system will process your results. You’ll receive an email with: Your Heart Health Risk Score (Low, Moderate, or High Risk). Personalized recommendations based on your responses. Information about how BeatAlert™ can support your heart health journey.
